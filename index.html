What is Dog-Gone?

It started when I was looking for an ID Tag for our dogs, I wanted one that would allow us to be contacted if they were found.

There are quite a few already on the market, but it seemed that they were all more interested in my details than my dogs! They all wanted me to pay them a subscription or to sign up to their app.

I just want to be contacted if someone finds my dog!

Dog-Gone QR Code

The Dog-Gone QR code image can be engraved into a metal tag to be attached to your dogs collar or harness.

When the tag is scanned on a mobile phone it will open a web page containing;

- Your dogs name

- Your phone number

- Second phone number (Optional)

When the QR Tag is scanned, the following screen will be displayed on the finders device, They can then choose to call the number displayed or click the button to send an SMS with their GPS location.

Using the QR Code?

It's simple, you can download the QR code image yourself and use it to create your tag, or you can order a tag from our Etsy store using the link provided on the QR page.

The only details you'll be asked to enter are;

Your dogs name and your contact number(s).

How it works

When the QR Tag is scanned, the following screen will be displayed on the finders device, They can then choose to call the number displayed or click the button to send an SMS with their GPS location.

Your details will only ever appear on the screen of the person who scans your tag. They're not captured by us, or shared in any way.
