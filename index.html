What is Dog-Gone?

It all started when I was looking for an ID Tag for our dogs that would allow us to be alerted if they were found.

Turns out there are quite a few already on the market, But it seemed that they were all more interested in my details, than my dogs!

They all wanted me to pay them a subscription or sign up to an app, which is great if you're into that kind of thing, but I just want to be contacted if someone finds my dog!

So, I decided to do it myself and being a nice person, I thought I'd share it for free.

The Dog-Gone QR Code

The Dog-Gone QR code can be engraved into a metal tag to be attached to your dogs collar or harness, when it's scanned on a mobile phone it will open a web page containing;

- Your dogs name

- Your phone number

- Second phone number (Optional)

The finder can then call you directly or they can click a button on the page to send you a text message with their current GPS location details.

How to use the QR Code?

It's simple, you just download the QR code image and use it to create your tag. The only details you'll be asked to enter are;

Your dogs name and your contact number(s).

Your details will only ever appear on the screen of the person who scans your tag. They're not captured by us, or shared in any way.

You can order a tag, either by using the link provided on the QR code page, or send it to any local engraving service to make a tag of your choice.
